Enhancing Geoscientific Workflows with MATLAB
 
This seminar provides an overview of what’s new in MATLAB, with specific focus on new functionality in mapping and the latest GenAI MATLAB Copilot features. 

Attendees will see examples that emphasize geospatial data analysis and use cases that highlight how MATLAB can streamline workflows and enhance productivity. 
 
MATLAB’s low-code, reproducible workflows enable researchers, instructors, and students who are new to programming while serving as a powerful, scalable development environment for veteran users.
